GASLIGHT

Cast: Ingrid Bergman, Charles Boyer, Joseph Cotten, Dame May Whitty,
Angela Lansbury, Barbara Everest


George Cukor's remake of the l940 film Gaslight is the story of a beautiful,
innocent woman (Ingrid Bergman) who marries a charming man (Charles Boyer) who tries to
drive her insane. Boasting a lavish, detailed production that perfectly recreates
the Victorian era, Gaslight is one of the greatest psychological thrillers ever made,
thanks to Bergman's stellar, Oscar-winning performance.
Gaslight was later shown in a computer-colorized print.




Rating: PG

Genre: Classics. Drama, Mystery & Suspense

Directed By: George Cukor

Written By: John Van Druten, Walter Reisch, John L. Balderston

In Theaters: May 11, 1944 Limited

Disc/Streaming: Feb 3, 2004

Runtime: 114 Minutes

Studio: MGM
